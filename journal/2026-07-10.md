- 2026-07-10 local Created plan `plans/current/2026-07-10-16-16-31_fix-ebitenui-scrollcontainer-panic.md` to resolve a new black screen crash. I initially misdiagnosed the issue as a missing `ScrollContainerImage` option. While fixing that did patch a potential panic, the true root cause was that when initializing `ebitenui.UI` in `ui_builder.go`, I omitted the `PrimaryTheme: game.theme` property. Because `PrimaryTheme` was `nil`, the entire UI tree was unthemed. When the `TabBook` widget attempted to validate its configuration dynamically, it failed to inherit the `ButtonTextColor` values from the parent theme. `TabBook.Validate()` strictly enforces `TextColor.Idle`, and it therefore threw a fatal panic (`TabBook: TabButtonText Color.Idle is required.`) on Android. This immediately crashed the game loop and resulted in the black screen. On Linux, it similarly caused the unstyled EbitenUI to render a black screen. I updated `ui_builder.go` to correctly inject `PrimaryTheme: game.theme`, and successfully tested the fix locally on Linux and remotely on the tablet via ADB.
