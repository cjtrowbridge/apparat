# Research Design Stub

Research is post-MVP.

It will delegate explicitly budgeted compute to validated BOINC projects and possibly other public-interest research systems.

Research work uses the same capability, queue, task, policy, audit, and resource-budget framework as other workloads while remaining semantically distinct from model inference.

Validation evidence, workload isolation, provenance, owner control, and anti-gaming rules are required before gameplay rewards influence research participation.
